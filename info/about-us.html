About Us

"Excellence Is The Secret Ingredient."

Founded in 1998 by a group of friends who shared a passion for food and a desire to create a truly unique dining experience.

Pizza Palace is a restaurant that combines traditional pizzeria warmth with a modern kitchen's creativity. Their menu includes a variety of pizzas, pastas, salads, and desserts made with quality local ingredients. They are known for their friendly service, welcoming atmosphere, and delicious food.

Enjoy Pizza in Any Mood

At Pizza Palace, we take great pride in crafting the perfect pie. We only use the freshest and highest-quality ingredients, from our signature sauce to our hand-tossed crust. No matter if you're in the mood for a classic cheese pizza or something more creative, we've got something for everyone.


Enjoy Pizza in Anywhere

Looking for the perfect pizza spot in Toronto? Look no further! Our 10 convenient locations across the city make it easy to enjoy delicious, high-quality pizza. Whether you're on-the-go or craving a night in, we've got you covered with our wide selection of pizza options. Come visit us at one of our locations and experience the ultimate pizza experience!

We are a Member of the Community

But we're not just about the pizza - we're part of the community. We know that good food brings people together, and we're proud to have been serving up delicious pizza to our friends and neighbors for many years. We want to give back by supporting local events, donating to local charities, and always being a friendly and welcoming presence in our community.

WHAT WE OFFER

At our pizza shop, we serve freshly made pasta that is crafted in-house, using only the best quality ingredients. Our selection of pasta includes four different kinds, which can be paired with a choice of eight delicious sauces made with freshest ingredients and no preservatives or additives. Our menu also features seasonal specials that are sure to delight your taste buds. End your meal with our decadent Buffalo Wings, a customer favorite!
